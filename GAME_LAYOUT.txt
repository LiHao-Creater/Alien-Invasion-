Color Scheme:
- Background: Light gray (230, 230, 230)
- Player Ship: Blue (0, 150, 255) - Triangle shape
- Aliens: Green (100, 255, 100) - Oval with eyes
- Player Bullets: Dark gray (60, 60, 60) - Thin rectangles
- Alien Bullets: Red (255, 0, 0) - Thin rectangles
- Shields: 
  * Full health: Green (0, 255, 0)
  * Medium damage: Yellow-green (200, 255, 0)
  * Heavy damage: Orange (255, 200, 0)
- Text: Dark gray (30, 30, 30)
- Button: Green (0, 255, 0) with white text

Game Mechanics:
1. Player can move left/right and fire up to 5 bullets at once
2. Aliens move side-to-side, dropping down when hitting edges
3. Aliens randomly fire bullets downward
4. Shields protect player but degrade with each hit (3 hits = destroyed)
5. Score increases with each alien destroyed (50 pts base, increases per level)
6. Game speeds up after clearing each wave
7. Player has 3 lives (ships)
8. Game over when all lives lost

Sound Effects:
- 🔊 Shoot: High-pitched tone (440 Hz) when firing
- 💥 Explosion: Mid-pitched tone (220 Hz) when alien destroyed
- 💔 Hit: Mid-high tone (330 Hz) when ship takes damage
